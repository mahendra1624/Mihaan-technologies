About Us

Mihaan technologies is a integrated audio visual and Digital surveillance system specialist. We take the latest technologies and provide simplified solutions for our clients across all segments and offer the full range service from Consultancy, Design to Installation and Systems Maintenance..

Our dedicated teams are the framework of who we are and how we succeed. Their loyalty reflects our ongoing commitment to offering great compensation, an engaging culture, and real opportunities for growth and success. We understand the challenges faced by our client’s and we enhance their productivity by integrating and simplifying cutting edge technologies.

Mihaan technologies was incorporated in 2017. Our core value involves offering a wholly in house service provision, ensuring at no point projects are outsourced. We also established an unrivalled reputation as one of the leading AV and Digital Surveillance solution provider, built on more innovative, quality and service.

Our service sector

Over the years, Mihaan technologies has delivered promising services for all the segments such as schools, institutions, colleges, universities, conference rooms, receptions, video walls, lobbies, homes and auditoriums etc.
